Pepperoni Flat Bread

Ingriedients:

  • Pepperoni
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Ragu Pizza Sauce
  • Whole Wheat Flat Bread

This recipe is simple, easy, and fun favorite for the entire family.
This can be enjoyed any day of the week with minimum effort!

Instructions:

  1. Spread tomato sauce on flat bread.
  2. Sprinkle 1 once of Mozorella Cheese
  3. Spread pepperoni all across entire flatbread
  4. Bake in over on 400 Degrees for seven minutes
